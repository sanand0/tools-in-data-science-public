# May 2026 Course Map

This is the working structure for the May 2026 term.

The course content is organized by week. Add new material into the sections below instead of placing lesson pages at the repository root.

## Sections

- [Weekly topics](weeks/README.md): Week 1 to Week 8 content and lesson links.
- [Assessments](assessments/README.md): graded assignments, exams, and grading policies.
- [Projects](projects/README.md): project briefs, evaluation details, and supporting files.
- [Resources](resources/README.md): setup, course links, tools, and reusable guidance.
- [Live sessions](live-sessions/README.md): term-specific session index.
